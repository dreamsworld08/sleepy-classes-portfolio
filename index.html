Behind the Educator

Shekhar Dutt is Co-Founder of Sleepy Classes IAS — one of India's leading online UPSC coaching platforms. With a BE (Mechanical Engineering) from PEC Chandigarh and MBA (Finance) from Panjab University, he is a self-taught Sociology expert with 16+ years of teaching experience.

He teaches Sociology Optional and GS Economy, and has guided students to AIR 2, AIR 5, AIR 13 and multiple single-digit UPSC ranks.
